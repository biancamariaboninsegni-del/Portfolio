Homo Faber 2024
The Journey of Life

A month-long biennial of contemporary craftsmanship held at the Fondazione Giorgio Cini in Venice, directed by Luca Guadagnino. As Brand & Communications Coordinator for the Michelangelo Foundation, I managed operational workflows, produced content for digital platforms and the international Young Ambassadors programme, and supported partnership and PR activities across 70+ countries for an event welcoming over 50,000 visitors.
